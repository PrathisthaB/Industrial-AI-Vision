What it does

A complete safety operations platform, not just a model.

🎥

Multi-source ingest

Webcam, uploaded video, and CCTV/RTSP streams all run through the same detection pipeline.

🪖

PPE detection

Identifies person, helmet, vest, and boots — and flags exactly which item is missing.

📸

Automatic evidence

Every violation is captured with a timestamped screenshot, confidence score, and camera ID.

📊

Live dashboard

Compliance percentages, active cameras, and recent alerts refresh continuously.

📈

Trend analytics

Daily, weekly, and monthly violation trends and compliance charts powered by Chart.js.

📄

Incident reports

One click turns a violation into a professional, exportable PDF incident report.

About the project

About

Industrial AI Vision started as a computer-vision prototype for PPE detection and has been rebuilt into a production-shaped safety platform: a layered Flask application (routes / services / models / database), session-based authentication, a persisted SQLite schema, a documented REST API, and generated incident reports — the same shape of system a plant EHS team would actually deploy.
